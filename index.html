About Our Wedding

A fitting tribute to the origin of our relationship, we will be getting married online.

From the exotic location of our backyard in Oakland, California, we will be streaming our wedding over Zoom in order to share it with you, our very special family and friends.

Since we sadly can’t be with you in person due to a global pandemic, we hope you will be join us to help celebrate our day, and together we will cross new frontiers in nuptial history.

Ben, Heather, and Baby Duncan.

The Fine Print

Here are some disclaimers before we begin:

  • You'll need a decent internet connection to participate. We will elaborate more on the technical recommendations below.
  • Dress code is fancy. Of course, we can really only see from the waist up, so if you want to wear yoga pants nobody will be any the wiser. If you don't feel like getting formal, that's fine, but we figure you're looking for an excuse to dress up right about now. Want to wear a costume? Sure, we can appreciate the frivolity.
  • This event is definitely BYOB. We could probably figure out a way to send you all drinks and food simultaneously, but we also have a 9-month-old baby and a matter of weeks to pull this all together. Next one's on us, we promise.
  • No travel necessary. We will all be enjoying the wedding from the comforts of our own home.

Schedule

All times are in Pacific Standard Timezone (PST).

We will be meeting privately with the County Clerk at 3:00 to perform the legal ceremony, but will join you after a short break at 4:00 p.m.

We ask that everyone be on Zoom before 4 p.m. so that any technical issues can be resolved.

  • 3:30-4:00 p.m. Zoom opens for guest entry.
  • 4:00 p.m. Reception begins.
  • 4:30 p.m.- 5:00 p.m. Trivia in breakout rooms.
  • 5:00 p.m.- 6:00 p.m. Dinner, cake, and toast.
  • 6:00 p.m.- 7:00 p.m. Dance Party!
  • 7:00 p.m. Farewell.

Starting at 4, your mics will be muted, and our DJ will be introduce us and get the party started. She will also be providing tech support throughout the evening, in case you are having issues. Again, I cannot stress enough, please arrive early so you can test out your mic and camera.

Feel free to chat in the Zoom chat with everyone! Laugh at baby's antics, criticize the dog's abysmal haircut, admire our backyard; whatever your heart desires.

We will be saving the chat so that we can read back through all of the comments we have missed (minus your private direct messages, of course). We won't be monitoring the chat; however, DJ Airsun will be keeping an eye on things if you'd like to be unmuted.

If you would like to give a short speech (5 minutes), there will be time during our toast. Since some of you may not have met, please introduce yourself and where you're from, since the usual opportunities to mingle won't exist. DJ Airsun will unmute you after you raise your hand.

Technical Recommendations

Here are some tips to help you enjoy the wedding as best as possible. If you need extra help, please reach out in advance to us (or really just Ben).

  • Use a device with a built-in camera. It’ll be more fun if we can see you, too! All smartphones, tablets, and most laptops fit the bill.
  • For a more comfortable viewing experience, use your device with the biggest display.
  • Use headphones when possible. Unless you’ll be sharing your device with others, a microphone or headphones with built-in mic will provide the best audio experience. Otherwise, use your built-in device speakers and mic.
  • Minimize the amount of background noise. You'll want to turn off anything that could be picked up by your microphone, like TVs, radios, vacuums, etc.
  • Download Zoom app. If attending via smartphone or tablet, download the Zoom app ahead of time. No account signup necessary.
  • Perform a network speed test Before the wedding, you should assess how your connection is performing. We recommend at least 1.5Mpbs up/down, and 3.0Mbps for HD video.
  • Find a stronger signal.If your speed isn’t performing as fast as you’d like, try positioning yourself closer to the wireless access point and test again.
  • Test with us ahead of time. We may set up a test session ahead of time for those of you who need to test out your setup. Please email/text for more information.
